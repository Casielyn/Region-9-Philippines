REGION IX

Region 9, also known as the Zamboanga Peninsula Region lies at the Southernmost portion of the Philippine archipelago. Located at the western tip of the island of Mindanao, Zamboanga Peninsula is strategically situated in close proximity to Sabah, Malaysia, Brunei Darussalam and Indonesia. Featured Artist

The term "featured artist" refers to the group or individual most prominently featured on a sound recording, track or album. A "non-featured artist" is an artist who is not prominently featured on a sound recording, track or album (i.e. a session musician or a back-up vocalist).

Festival

A festival is a gathering of people to celebrate something. It can also refer to a one day or more when when people in a country have a holiday so that they can celebrate something. Festivals may be religious or national.
